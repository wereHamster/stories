I can’t afford not working for long periods of time, I’d eventually run out of
money. But I’m fortunate enough that I can work from wherever there is good
internet. And that covers a lot of this earths surface (and airspace).

I’m also pretty flexible when it comes to working hours. This gives me plenty of
options how to structure my day.

If it’s raining I can stay home and work a bit more. If it’s cloudy outside I
can go work at a cafe. If there’s thermal activity I can go flying. If I don’t
feel productive I can… chill.

My original plan was to go to Morocco, stay for a few weeks, work, travel, fly.
It was difficult to find a country that is in the right time zone such that I
don’t have to get up in the middle of the night for meetings, has good internet,
is warm and cheap, and is flyable during that time of year.

I only made it to the check-in where the airline informed me that Morocco
doesn’t want tourists after all. Oh well, such is life and travel in corona
times...

Back to the drawing board. Tenerife was open to tourists, and winter is the best
time of the year to fly. Booked!

I wasn't expecting many tourists on the island, it was off-season after all. But
I saw way even fewer than even that. Could there be another reason why the
island was that deserted?

Later more people started pouring onto the island. Though the island is big
enough such that there are always places that one can retreat to.

I watched so many sunsets. At one point I almost thought I’ve had enough.
Almost. Because whenever I saw the next one, it was different. Unique in its own
way. And every afternoon I found myself looking forward to the next one.

Cider, dark chocolate, feet buried in the warm sand, a cold breeze in my face.

Climate difference between north and south, sea level and 3500amsl. Clouds at
1000m, above clear skies.

COVID, masks.

Young people from Europe been staying there sind juli, November, February.

Back tu zurich: no Snow, Green trees. Ppl everywhere.

Getting back home, COVID test.

How should the story feel? What emotions should it evoke?
